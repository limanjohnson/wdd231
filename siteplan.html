Site Name

Lead Vault

This name has been chosen to keep sales in mind.

Purpose

The idea of this app is to create a very basic way of adding potential clients (leads) to a web app. Later a user can view leads and see their contact information. They can schedule events they would like to complete.

Scenarios

  1. What is a simple and quick way for me to organize contact information for new people I meet?
  2. What is a simple and quick way for me to plan to contact people I have met?
